Frequently Asked Garden Maintenance Questions

How often should I maintain my garden?

The frequency of garden maintenance depends on the types of plants in your garden and the season. Generally, routine maintenance is needed every 1-2 weeks during peak growing seasons, with less frequent care during the winter months.

What's the best way to water my garden?

Consistent watering is key to a healthy garden, but it's important not to overwater. Deep, infrequent watering encourages strong root systems. The best time to water is early in the morning or late in the evening when temperatures are cooler to avoid evaporation.

How do I control pests in my garden?

To control pests, we use organic and eco-friendly pest control methods that protect your plants while ensuring a safe environment for your business. Regular inspections, removing infected plants, and using natural deterrents can help keep pests at bay.
